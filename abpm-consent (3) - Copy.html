24-Hour Ambulatory Blood
Pressure Monitoring

Please read all sections carefully and complete each field below. Your signed consent will be submitted directly to the practice. If you have any questions, contact us before completing this form.

Purpose

A portable blood pressure monitor worn for 24 hours to gather readings during your normal daily activities and sleep, providing a more accurate picture than a single clinic reading.

Benefits

Supports more accurate diagnosis and management of hypertension and related conditions by capturing blood pressure variation throughout the day and night.

Procedure

  • Cuff placed on your upper arm
  • Readings every 30 mins (day) and 60 mins (night)
  • Normal routine can continue
  • Keep monitor dry, avoid vigorous exercise

Confidentiality

All data is confidential and stored securely. Only authorised clinical staff at Kinsealy Medical Centre will access your information.
