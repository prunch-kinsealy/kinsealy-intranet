Skincare Advice

Please read before starting treatment

Isotretinoin dramatically reduces oil production — effective for acne, but your skin, lips, eyes, and nose will become dry. Start a simple skincare routine 1–2 weeks before your first dose to protect your skin barrier from the outset.
🛑 Stop All Active Products Now
Retinol / retinoid creams
Salicylic / glycolic / lactic acid
Benzoyl peroxide
Vitamin C serums
Scrubs & exfoliating brushes

Use only fragrance-free, gentle products. Your routine should be: cleanser · moisturiser · sunscreen · lip balm. If a product tingles, stings, or smells strong — stop using it.
